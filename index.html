Prioritization

What you don't build matters more than what you do

The kill decision is the hardest and most revealing thing a PM does. Judgment shows up in the decision to stop.

Activation

Activation and onboarding are different problems

Onboarding gets users in the door. Activation proves they found value. The two need separate instrumentation and separate ownership.

AI Product

AI in product: earn it or it earns you

AI belongs in a product when it closes a gap the user has. A team that starts from "where can we add AI" will always find an answer and rarely a good one.
